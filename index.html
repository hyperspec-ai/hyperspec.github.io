Our Technology

Hyperspectral Point Cloud

Hyperspectral point clouds integrate spatial (depth) information and spectral (reflectivity) data, providing a comprehensive dataset for geospatial analysis. This allows for precise mapping of terrain and infrastructure while identifying materials based on their unique spectral signatures. Such detailed information is invaluable in applications like environmental monitoring, urban planning, disaster management, and regulatory audits. By combining the physical layout with material properties, hyperspectral point clouds enhance the accuracy and efficiency of analyzing and managing natural and built environments.

Embodied AI

Multimodal data from vehicles, including visual, LiDAR, radar, and GPS information, enhances embodied AI robots' capabilities by providing a comprehensive understanding of their environment. Visual data aids in object recognition, while LiDAR and radar offer depth and motion information for navigation and obstacle avoidance. GPS data ensures precise localization, crucial for tasks requiring geospatial awareness. This combination creates robust situational awareness, enabling AI robots to operate more effectively and autonomously in dynamic settings.
